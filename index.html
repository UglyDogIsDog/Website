 INTRODUCTION 


I am currently an undergraduate at Zhejiang University (ZJU) supervised by Prof. Hongxin Zhang. I will receive my B.Eng. in software engineering from ZJU this June. Later I will go for the Ph.D. degree of computer science at the University of Wisconsin - Madison, under the supervision of Prof. Michael Gleicher.

My research interest is information visualization and deep learning. My current research subject is analyzing data with visualization and human-computer interaction. I like new pieces of stuff - new tech and new life. My hobbies are much broader than my competence, including watching movies, reading books, and of course, coding.
